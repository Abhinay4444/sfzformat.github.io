title: cutoff_ccN
---
The variation in the cutoff frequency
when MIDI continuous controller N is received, in cents.

##### Example

```
cutoff_cc1=1200
cutoff_cc2=-100
```

| Type    | Default | Range               |
| ---     | ---     | ---                 |
| integer | 0       | -9600 to 9600 cents |
