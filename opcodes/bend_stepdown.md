lang: en
title: bend_stepdown
---
Pitch bend step, in cents, for downward pitch bends.

##### Examples

```
bend_stepdown=100 // glissando in semitones
bend_stepdown=200 // glissando in whole tones
bend_stepdown=1200 //leap an entire octave at a time
```

| Type    | Default | Range     |
| ---     | ---     | ---       |
| integer | 1       | 1 to 1200 |
