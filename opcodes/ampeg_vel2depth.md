Velocity effect on EG depth, in cents for pitch or filter cutoff.

##### Examples

```
pitcheg_vel2depth=1200
fileg_vel2depth=4000
```

Range from -12000 to 12000 cents. 12000 cents is 10 octaves.

| Type  | Default | Range                 |
| ---   | ---     | ---                   |
| float | 0       | -12000 to 12000 cents |
