---
lang: en
title: amp_keycenter
---
Center key for amplifier keyboard tracking. In this key, the amplifier keyboard
tracking will have no effect.

##### Examples

```
amp_keycenter=60

amp_keycenter=48
```

| Type    | Default | Range    |
| ---     | ---     | ---      |
| integer | 60      | 0 to 127 |
