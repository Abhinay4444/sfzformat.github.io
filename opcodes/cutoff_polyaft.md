---
title: cutoff_polyaft
---
The variation in the cutoff frequency when
MIDI polyphonic aftertouch messages are received, in cents.

##### Examples

```
cutoff_polyaft=1200

cutoff_polyaft=-100
```

| Type    | Default | Range               |
| ---     | ---     | ---                 |
| integer | 0       | -9600 to 9600 cents |
