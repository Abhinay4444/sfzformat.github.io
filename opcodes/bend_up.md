---
lang: en
title: bend_up
---
Pitch bend range when Bend Wheel or Joystick is moved up, in cents.

If `bend_up` is negative,
then moving the pitch wheel up will cause the pitch to move down.

##### Examples

```
bend_up=1200

bend_up=100
```

| Type    | Default | Range               |
| ---     | ---     | ---                 |
| integer | 200     | -9600 to 9600 cents |
